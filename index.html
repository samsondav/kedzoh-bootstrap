How it Works

Kedzoh is an employee training platform that allows employers to create 3-5 minute training sessions for their employees. Lessons can be created easily on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Kedzoh offers the ability to teach employees and managers about any aspect of your business with the simple click of the “Next” button. At the end of the lesson there are short quizzes and a rating system that show an employer which employees have taken the quizzes, what they learned from it, and the enjoyment they received from it.

Benefits of Kedzoh

The implementation of Kedzoh allows employees to be taught regardless of where they are, reducing the amount of time needed to train each individual employee. With Kedzoh there is no need to have training course that take a week; short training lessons can be sent out multiple times per day. Our platform will allow your company to increase training effectiveness and reduces the waste of time and money.
